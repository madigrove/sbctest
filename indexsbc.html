Volunteer Opportunities

Our programs would not be able to happen without the amazing efforts of our Red Shirt Volunteers!

The Creatives

Red Shirt Volunteers are needed to help presenters and students in the hands-on sessions, assist with lunch, guide students at the University of Minnesota, and ensure that students have a fun and exciting day!

Knowledge Bowl

Volunteers are needed to help run the competition by reading questions or judging during rounds. New volunteers will be paired with an experienced volunteer or coach. On-site training is provided before the meet begins.

Young Authors Conference

Red Shirt Volunteers are needed to assist with lunch, guide students around Bethel University, monitor the book fair and open mic, and ensure that students have a fun and exciting day!

STEMLink

Red Shirt Volunteers are needed as Team Leaders and Classroom Assistants as well as a Lunch Team. This is a fast-paced high-energy day where students and volunteers will learn something new. No experience required, just an interest in STEM and working with kids.

In-Office Assistance

Want to volunteer, but can't to commit to a long day? We have a LOT of nuts and bolts to every program we coordinate. We always have various tasks that need to be completed to have a succesful program. This could involve organizing mailings to the hundreds of schools, running student/teacher evaluations, repairing signs, assemble volunteer packets, and much more!
